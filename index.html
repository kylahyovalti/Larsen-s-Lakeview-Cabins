Welcome to Larsen's Lakeview Cabins!

Our cabins are located 2 miles northeast of Grand Marais on the shore of beautiful Lake Superior. Our cabins are seasonal and are available from May - October. Every cabin has a spectacular view of Lake Superior and offer a cozy retreat while visiting the North Shore. They are completely furnished and have kitchens, showers and microwaves. Each cabin also has a picnic table, grill, and firepit to enjoy during your stay.

There are many activities close by. Try fishing, hunting, shopping, hiking & biking trails, golfing, sightseeing and many others!

Lawrence Larsen built the Lakeview Cabins in 1932. He was a commerical fisherman for many years. His fish house and winch house still stands today!

We hope that our site will increase our effectiveness in meeting new customers and responding to existing ones. Included is information on our accommodations and location, with pictures and descriptions.

Don't hesitate to reach out, customer satisfaction is extremely important to us!
